## Paneer Butter Masala

**Ingredients:**  
- 250g paneer (cubed)  
- 3 tomatoes (pureed)  
- 1 large onion (finely chopped)  
- 1/2 cup cream  
- 2 tbsp butter  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 1 tsp red chili powder  
- 1 tsp garam masala  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dry fenugreek leaves)  
- Salt to taste  
- Fresh coriander for garnish

**Instructions:**  
1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.  
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.  
3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.  
4. Crush kasuri methi between your palms and add along with garam masala.  
5. Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Cook for 5 minutes.  
6. Stir in cream and simmer for another 3-4 minutes.  
7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

## Chole Bhature

**Ingredients for Chole (Chickpea Curry):**  
- 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)  
- 2 onions (finely chopped)  
- 2 tomatoes (pureed)  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 2 green chilies (slit)  
- 1 tsp cumin seeds  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 1 tbsp chole masala  
- 1 tsp red chili powder  
- Salt to taste  
- Fresh coriander for garnish

**Instructions:**  
1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt and turmeric until soft.  
2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then sauté onions until golden.  
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for a minute.  
4. Add tomato puree, chole masala, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.  
5. Add cooked chickpeas along with water as needed. Simmer for 15 minutes.  
6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

**Ingredients for Bhature:**  
- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 1/2 cup yogurt  
- 1 tsp sugar  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- Water (as needed)  
- Oil for frying

**Instructions:**  
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and yogurt to form a soft dough.  
2. Let it rest for 2 hours.  
3. Roll into medium-sized discs and deep fry in hot oil until puffed and golden.  
4. Serve bhature hot with chole.

## Masala Maggi

**Ingredients:**  
- 1 packet Maggi noodles  
- 1 onion (finely chopped)  
- 1 tomato (finely chopped)  
- 1/2 capsicum (chopped)  
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 1/2 tsp turmeric  
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder  
- 1/2 tsp garam masala  
- Salt to taste  
- 1 1/2 cups water  
- 1 tbsp oil

**Instructions:**  
1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until translucent.  
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.  
3. Add tomatoes and capsicum; cook until soft.  
4. Add turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt; mix well.  
5. Add water and bring to boil.  
6. Add Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.  
7. Add the Maggi tastemaker masala, mix, and cook until water is absorbed.  
8. Serve hot.

## Grilled Sandwich

**Ingredients:**  
- 4 slices bread  
- 1/2 cup mixed veggies (onions, capsicum, tomatoes, boiled potatoes) finely chopped  
- 2 tbsp butter  
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- 1 tsp chaat masala (optional)

**Instructions:**  
1. Mix veggies with salt, pepper, and chaat masala.  
2. Butter one side of each bread slice.  
3. Place one slice butter-side-down on the pan or sandwich maker.  
4. Add the mixed veggies and sprinkle cheese on top.  
5. Cover with another slice butter-side-up.  
6. Grill until golden brown and cheese melts.  
7. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
